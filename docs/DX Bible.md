1\. Title & Premise

Title:

DREAMXTREME: TRUTH DEBT – LAKE ELSINORE

Premise:

You come to on the cracked shoulder of Lakeshore Drive in Lake Elsinore, ears ringing, clothes damp with lake water and mystery soda, with a half-remembered dream and a needle-sunk grapefruit bobbing somewhere behind your eyes. 

Your only guide is FEELZ, a janky therapy app on your phone, and the people you meet around the lake: junkies, church ladies, queer artists, bikers, and city functionaries all clinging to their own favorite lies. 

Every time you swipe right to tell a comforting lie, you stabilize the moment but add to a hidden Truth Debt. Every time you swipe left to let the truth in, something cracks now instead of later.

The setting is explicitly Lake Elsinore—but in-world, the rebrand/civic myth calls it “Lake Ulysses” on the signs and ads: a “3,000 acre jewel” that keeps having toxic algae “incidents” that are always “under control.”

	•	Center Card:

	•	FEELZ Emotion Wheel button (tap to pick which emotion “colors” your response)

	•	Tiny pulsing Truth Debt sigil that glitches harder as Debt rises

3.2 Decision Flow per Card

	1\.	Tap FEELZ → wheel pops up over lower half of the screen.

	•	Choose the emotion you’re leading with (e.g., Anger vs Concern).

	•	Wheel evolves over the run: from chunky pixels to sharper, unlocking more nuanced emotions, exactly like your doc. 

	2\.	Swipe the card:

	•	Left ≈ truth / confrontation / naming what’s really happening

	•	Right ≈ lie / smoothing over / going along with a narrative

As you drag:

	•	Up/down arrows flicker over the four meters

	•	The Debt sigil flickers if it’s a lie

	3\.	Release → consequences:

	•	If it’s a lie-swipe:

	•	Integrity –

	•	Stability and/or Trust \+ (short-term win)

	•	Truth Debt \+1–5 based on card’s internal “lie size”

	•	That lie is logged in a run-specific Truth Ledger (you can view it anytime).

	•	If it’s a truth-swipe:

	•	Integrity \+

	•	Lucidity \+

	•	Trust/Stability may drop or spike depending on the NPC / context

	•	No new Debt, but you might “pop” existing lies into mini-Reckonings.

As Debt passes thresholds (10, 25, 40, 60…), Lake Elsinore visually degrades:

	•	Pixel water turns sickly, more dead fish sprites, glitching advisory banners, etc., paralleling real HAB “danger” advisories where the lake swings between “open” and “don’t touch the water at all.”

5\. Truth Debt & Final Reckoning (Lake Elsinore Flavor)

As you accumulate lies:

	•	Truth Debt thresholds spawn “Bloom Events”:

	•	Fish kills, glowing green water tiles, advisory banners…

	•	NPCs repeat lines back at you with subtle changes, as if they remember contradictory versions.

When you hit max Debt or a key story checkpoint:

5.1 Council / Beautification Hearing

You get pulled into a Downtown Beautification / Lake Reopening meeting at City Hall.

	•	This is your Reckoning: a series of swipe cards built from your logged lies.

	•	Each card calls back one specific lie (who, where, what you said) with two swipes:

	•	Confess (left) – Truth, higher immediate pain, pays off that lie’s portion of Debt, sometimes burns relationships or stability.

	•	Double Down (right) – Bigger lie, temporary Stability, but multiplies remaining Debt and visibly worsens the algae / fire / building-collapse visuals.

We end with your “historic downtown” pseudo-nostalgia literally on fire, echoing your “historic building on fire” trailer note.

Use your previous ending types, but framed through Lake Elsinore:

	•	Clean Cut:

The lake is closed again. Downtown is half-boarded, but the water is finally clearing. You told the truth and paid in full.

	•	Functional Mask:

Each ending unlocks new Masks (Therapist / Martyr / Optimist / Child) and maybe new FEELZ wheel slices.
